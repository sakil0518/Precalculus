Graphs of equations

Frequently, a relationship between two quantities is expressed as an equation in two variables. For instant, y=7-3x is an equation in x and y. An ordered pair (a,b) is a solution or solution point of an equation in x and y if the equation is true when a is substitute for x and b is substitute for y.
